Fr - Mapping and Visualisation

What OpenDC can do

Fr - OpenDC mainly uses the QGIS framework as a GIS tool. With QGIS, OpenDC can provide communicative maps as well as perform advanced GIS analysis such as the detection of catchment areas but also advanced routing features. OpenDC can also provide GIS training in general and on QGIS in particular.
