Assistance on process automation

What OpenDC can do

OpenDC mainly uses open-source tools and languages but stays pragmatic when a language or an application is popular but not open-source. Therefore OpenDC also uses the VBA language to automate processes on any Microsoft Office application. Python, C++ and SQL are used for any other application.

How this service can help your organisation

OpenDC can help you use automate your processes if you are working for a

  • Transport operator and have to deal with quantity of data every day, repeating a lot of manual processes.


  • Research programme with limited programming skills among your team and need to process a big quantity of data and information.


  • Consultancy performing manual tasks manually and would like a custom piece of software to automate these processes.


  • Any business that wastes time every day repeating the same operations on the Web, Excel, databases, text files etc. or that does not offer some services because of the time it would take to process these operations. The process automation would save your time spent on tedious tasks, enable you to focus on the creative parts of your work and inspire you to offer to products to your clients.
