Assistance on mobility studies

What OpenDC can do

OpenDC’s founder has over 10 years experience in transport and pedestrian modelling projects, working in London, Paris and Guinea Conakry. OpenDC can help strengthen your team on high profile mobility projects with advanced modelling and project management skills.

How this service can help your organisation

OpenDC can help you in your mobility project if you are working for a

  • Local council and want to be advised on a mobility study you would like to launch.
  • Non Governmental Organisation (NGO) and would like some mobility expertise for a project you are running.
  • Transport operator and need an experienced modeller to assist you in a specific project.
  • Research programme and need skills and experience in the collection of mobility data, using open-data and advanced transport APIs.
  • Consultancy specialised in traffic but not pedestrian modelling or reverse and need to strengthen your team with complementary skills.
  • Urban planner that needs a mobility engineer to partner with in your project.
  • Lender, key donor or funder that needs advanced mobility analysis to understand and demonstrate links between development and general accessibility.
  • Property investor who would like to estimate transport accessibility in the present but also in the future, taking into account transport projects, modelling future access times and understand how attractive future property location will be.
  • Retailer who wants to understand how the accessibility to their shopping centre can be improved.
  • Any business that needs expertise in mobility studies, from the analysis of survey data, to the modelling of future transportation networks, demand forecasts and general travel time and accessibility analysis.
