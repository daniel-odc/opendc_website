OpenDC - Open Data Consultants

Data analysis, model development and mobility studies, based on Open Data and Open Source tools and languages.

About us

OpenDC is a start-up specialised in data analysis and mobility studies. OpenDC mainly works with open data sets and open source languages.

OpenDC helps businesses and public bodies find open data that can help them get new insights, be more efficient or develop new products. We help find, download, store, analyse and visualise the data. We use mainly open source tools (but are not limited to them) and can provide our client with the source code and tools developed to deliver our product. OpenDC also has an extensive experience in the analysis of mobility data such as mobility survey data, GPS and mobile phone data and can assist on mobility studies and model development.

Our work

Our work experience covers a wide range of disciplines and applications. We have been involved in open data analysis, GPS and mobile phone data, transport modelling and software development projects.
